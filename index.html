How it works

Four stages stand between
your family and the air outside.

01
Pre-Filter
The first line of defence captures large particles — dust, hair, pet dander — protecting the filters below and extending their life.
02
True HEPA H13
Medical-grade filtration removes 99.97% of particles down to 0.3 microns. PM2.5, pollen, mould spores, and fine smoke — gone.
03
Activated Carbon
A thick carbon layer absorbs VOCs, formaldehyde, benzene, and the cooking odours specific to Indian homes. The air you smell becomes the air you breathe.
04
PlasmaWave™
WINIX's patented technology generates natural hydroxyl radicals — the same molecules the atmosphere uses — to neutralise viruses, bacteria, and chemical compounds at the molecular level.
